NEMAC National Environmental Modeling and Analysis Center

NEMAC is a center within the University of North Carolina Asheville and is funded completely by grants and contracts with outside agencies. These partners value working with a university center that has a range of expertise to deliver science to the public. Creating tools for delivering science to decision makers and the process of making decisions based on data drives each of the focus areas.
